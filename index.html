Info

Looking to jump start your CS career? Trying to boost your resume before the career fair? Gearing up for HackGT 6 and other hackathons?

Look no further! HackGT presents HackGTeeny, a series of beginner workshops to introduce critical skills for application development. Hosted by Georgia Tech's favorite hackathon, we're offering a 7 hour, hands-on, mentor-guided grind for students with some (but not extensive) coding experience.

Workshops will cover design, mobile app development, and web development!

FAQs

  • Who is eligible?

    As long as you're a current Georgia Tech student, you're eligible. No prior experience is required. Confirmations will be sent out on September 13th.

  • What should I bring?

    Bring your laptop (and charger!), headphones, and anything else you need to work comfortably. We'll supply lunch. If you're still not sure, ask us on Facebook or email!

  • Where do I go?

    HackGTeeny will take place in CULC 152.
